Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

🔍 1. Overview

Welcome to Pill Reminder & Medication Tracker. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information in compliance with Google Play Store requirements.

💾 2. Data Collection & Storage

Local-First Data: All your medication schedules, history, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using a local database (Dexie / IndexedDB). We do not transmit, upload, or store your health data on any external servers.

No Personal Information: We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, or phone number to use the core features of the app.

📢 3. Third-Party Services

Advertising (AdMob): We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use anonymous data, such as device identifiers (Advertising ID), to serve personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

🔐 4. Permissions

The app requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Notifications — To send you timely medication reminders.
  • Vibration — To alert you during alarms.

🗑️ 5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device as long as the app is installed. You can delete all your data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

🧒 6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
